Fredrik Backman

Fredrik Backman is a Swedish author whose warm, character-driven novels have captivated millions worldwide. His stories—from the curmudgeonly A Man Called Ove to the hockey-obsessed town of Beartown—blend sharp wit with deep empathy, often exploring family, community, and the small acts of kindness that define us. With over twenty million copies sold in dozens of languages and film adaptations including the Tom Hanks–led A Man Called Otto, Backman has become one of Scandinavia’s most beloved storytellers, writing in a voice that feels both distinctly Nordic and universally human.
